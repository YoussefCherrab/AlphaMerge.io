How to Play

Slide letter tiles on a 4×4 grid. Two matching letters merge into the next — A+A=B, all the way to Z. Reach Z to win!

Controls

⌨ Keyboard

Arrow keys ← → ↑ ↓.

👆 Swipe

Swipe anywhere on the board.

↩ Undo

3 undos per game. Badge shows remaining.

Merge Chain

  • A+A=B · B+B=C · … · Y+Y=Z 🎉
  • One merge per tile per move.

Win / Lose

  • Win: Create a Z tile — keep playing to beat your score!
  • Lose: Board full, no merges possible.

Moves

Every valid swipe counts as one move. Try to reach Z in as few moves as possible! Best shows the highest letter you ever created.

Letter & Color Reference

Tips

  • Keep your highest tile in a corner — never slide it away.
  • Build a descending chain along one edge so merges cascade.
  • Save undos for emergencies, not routine mistakes.

About AlphaMerge

AlphaMerge is a free browser puzzle game created by Youssef Cherrab. Instead of numbers you advance through the 26 letters of the alphabet.

Why Letters?

Everyone knows A, B, C — the chain is immediately intuitive and accessible to all ages.

Technology

Pure HTML, CSS & JavaScript. No frameworks. Installable as a PWA. Scores stored in localStorage.
